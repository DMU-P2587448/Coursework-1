The Dark Knight Rises Cast

Christian Bale

His career as an actor, Christian Bale, started when he was just a boy. He starred in Empire of the Sun; the movie was praised by most critics. In the 1990’s he participated in the production of Disney’s musical drama Newsies, as well as lent his voice in the animated film Pocahontas. In 1999, Bale portrayed Jesus of Nazareth in a television production titled, Mary, Mother of Jesus. He has also starred in various television dramas, in some more successful than others. He has played with actors such as: Nicolas Cage, Penelope Cruz, Samuel L. Jackson, and others. Christian Bale has also been awarded numerous times at the Academy Awards for, among others, Best Supporting Actor and Best Actor.

Michael Cain

Michael Caine started his actor career in the 1950’s where he played small and mostly uncredited films made in Britain. In 1964 he played a role in the film Zulu which was a breakthrough for the actor as he gained recognition as the most famous actor of the 1960’s. Caine has been nominated for the Academy Awards for Best Actor after playing in the movie Sleuth in 1972, later in 1986 and 1999 he won Best Supporting Actor Oscars for playing in the movies Hannah and Her Sisters and The Cider House Rules. He had also taken part in the production of Inception (2010) and Interstellar (2014) both directed by Christopher Nolan, a British-American director, who was also responsible for The Dark Night Trilogy.

Morgan Freeman

Morgan Freeman began his on-screen career when he appeared on a 1971 children's program called The Electric Company. He got his first recognition after playing a role in the family drama Who Says I Can't Ride a Rainbow! which also starred Jack Klugman. He gained worldwide recognition in 1989 when three of his films, Glory, Driving Miss Daisy and Lean on Me, were released. He has performed with stars such as Clint Eastwood, Kevin Costner, and Denzel Washington. He has also won numerous Academy Awards for, among others, Best Actor and Best Supporting Actor.
